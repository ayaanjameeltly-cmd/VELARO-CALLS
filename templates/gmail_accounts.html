Should I warm up first?

Yes — absolutely. New Gmail accounts flagged for cold outreach get banned fast.

Week 1–2: only send to people you know (friends, personal contacts). No cold emails.

Week 3: start mixing in PI leads slowly (15–20/day).

Week 4+: ramp up following the schedule.

The system enforces daily limits automatically based on account age.
